What I do.

Started in PHP, building full-stack web applications from the ground up. Over time I moved deeper into the backend — Node.js, TypeScript, distributed systems — and from there into blockchain, writing production smart contracts for Ethereum and EVM-compatible chains. Eight years in, always on the server side of things.

01

Backend Development

Node.js and TypeScript are where I spend most of my time. NestJS microservices, scalable REST APIs and the kind of server-side architecture that handles real load — built for production from day one.

02

Blockchain & Smart Contracts
